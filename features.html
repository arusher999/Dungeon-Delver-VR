Key Game Features

  • VR Dungeon Exploration: Navigate dark corridors and vast chambers using intuitive VR controls.
  • Combat Mechanics: Battle a variety of enemies with melee or ranged attacks, leveraging Roblox’s physics and VR interactions.
  • Puzzle Solving: Unlock hidden pathways and secrets through clever puzzle designs.
  • Procedural Generation: Randomized dungeons for unique experiences every session.
  • Progression & Rewards: Collect rare artifacts, upgrade equipment, and level up your avatar.
  • Immersive Environment: Engage with dynamic lighting, spatial audio, and haptic feedback to heighten immersion.

Project Roadmap Highlights

Our development milestones include:

  • Environment Design (1–2 weeks): Build the fundamental dungeon structure and basic interactive elements.
  • VR Mechanics Implementation (2 weeks): Develop locomotion, hand interactions, and combat systems.
  • AI & Procedural Generation (1.5 weeks): Integrate enemy behaviors and random dungeon layouts.
  • UI & Player Experience (1 week): Create HUD elements, tutorials, and in-game prompts.
  • Testing & Iteration (Ongoing): Continuously refine and optimize performance, comfort, and gameplay flow.
